Our Story

Rooted in purpose,
bottled with care.

COCOBARN was born from a simple belief: Thai coconut farmers deserve more value for what they grow. Sourcing from 100% organic Thai namhom coconut farms that share our vision for sustainability, we harvest only at the best timing, care for the land, and turn organic by-products back to the cycle—working toward near-zero waste.

The "O" in COCOBARN is intentionally imperfect to symbolize our coconuts that are never identical. Even their natural sweetness could vary. And that's exactly what makes them real, natural, and at their best.
